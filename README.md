# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cherry Auto Discounts (operated by Trong Nguyen) (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when merchants install or use **Cherry Auto Discounts** (the “App”) on Shopify.

The App helps Shopify merchants sync eligible automatic discounts, calculate storefront discount display values, and write product variant metafields so discount information can be shown on product pages.

## Information We Collect

### Information collected through Shopify APIs

When a merchant installs or uses the App, we may collect and process the following information from Shopify:

- Store information, including the shop domain.
- Shopify app session and authentication information, including access tokens, refresh tokens, granted scopes, token expiry information, and session state.
- Merchant/admin user information associated with the Shopify session, such as user ID, name, email address, locale, account owner status, collaborator status, and email verification status.
- Discount information, including automatic discount IDs, discount titles, discount type, discount value, status, start and end dates, combination settings, eligibility status, and the products, variants, or collections to which a discount applies.
- Product catalog information needed to calculate discount displays, including product IDs, variant IDs, collection IDs, and variant prices.
- App-generated display information, including whether a discount display is shown or hidden, original price, discounted price, discount label, linked discount ID, activation time, hidden time, and calculation time.

### Information collected directly from merchants

We may collect information that merchants provide directly to us, such as:

- Support requests and related contact information.
- Information included in communications with us.
- Any business or technical information voluntarily provided while requesting help with the App.

### Logs and technical information

Our hosting, database, and logging systems may generate technical logs, which can include timestamps, request paths, store domain, webhook topics, error messages, IP addresses, browser or device information, and other operational data needed to secure, operate, debug, and improve the App.

### Customer information

The App is not designed to collect or store customer personal information. The App does not intentionally collect customer names, email addresses, phone numbers, addresses, order data, cart data, checkout data, payment data, or customer browsing behavior.

If Shopify sends us a customer data request or customer redaction request, our current App behavior is to process the request as one for which we do not store customer personal data.

## Shopify API Permissions

The App requests the following Shopify API permissions:

- `read_discounts`: to read automatic discount configuration.
- `read_products`: to read product, variant, collection, and pricing information needed to calculate discount displays.
- `write_products`: to write and update product variant metafields used to show or hide discount display information.

We use these permissions only to provide and maintain the App’s discount display functionality.

## How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate merchants and authorized Shopify admin users.
- Maintain secure App sessions.
- Sync eligible Shopify automatic discounts.
- Determine whether discounts can be displayed by the App.
- Calculate original prices, discounted prices, and discount labels for product variants.
- Create, update, or remove Shopify product variant metafields used for storefront discount displays.
- Show merchants the current status of discounts in the App.
- Provide support and troubleshoot errors.
- Monitor security and prevent unauthorized access.
- Comply with legal obligations and Shopify platform requirements, including mandatory privacy and compliance webhooks.

We do not use customer personal information for advertising, profiling, resale, or unrelated purposes.

## Cookies and Tracking

The App may use strictly necessary session or authentication technologies required for Shopify embedded app functionality, security, and login flows.

We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies, behavioral tracking pixels, or analytics tools to track customers across stores or websites. Shopify and the merchant’s Shopify admin may use their own cookies or similar technologies under Shopify’s policies.

## Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information only as needed with:

- Shopify, to operate the App through Shopify APIs and comply with Shopify platform requirements.
- Hosting, database, infrastructure, logging, and security providers that help us run the App.
- Support or business service providers, if needed to respond to merchant requests.
- Legal, regulatory, or government authorities if required by law.
- A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of business assets, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.

Our service providers are authorized to use information only as necessary to provide services to us.

## Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain security, and enforce agreements.

When a merchant uninstalls the App, we delete Shopify session records associated with the shop when the uninstall webhook is received. When Shopify sends a shop redaction request, we delete remaining shop-specific App data, including stored discount source records, storefront display records, product discount display records, and session records.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information processed by the App, including Shopify OAuth authentication, access controls, HTTPS, and restricted access to production systems.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Merchant And User Rights

Depending on applicable law, merchants and authorized users may have rights to request:

- Access to personal information we hold about them.
- Correction of inaccurate information.
- Deletion of information.
- A copy or export of information.
- Restriction of or objection to certain processing.

To make a request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify the request before responding.

For requests involving Shopify customer personal data, merchants and customers should also use Shopify’s privacy request processes. The App does not currently store customer personal data.

## Automated Processing

The App automatically calculates product variant discount display values based on Shopify discount rules and product prices. These calculations do not use customer personal information and are not used for profiling or automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on individuals.

## Children

The App is intended for Shopify merchants and authorized business users. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and, where appropriate, notify merchants through the App, by email, or through another reasonable method.
